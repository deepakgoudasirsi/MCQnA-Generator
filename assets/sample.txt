Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the Indian state of Karnataka and is one of the country's most dynamic and rapidly growing cities. Known as the "Silicon Valley of India," it has earned a global reputation for its thriving tech industry, hosting numerous IT companies, startups, and research institutions.
Beyond its tech-driven economy, Bangalore boasts a rich cultural heritage, with a blend of modern infrastructure and historical landmarks.
The city is also renowned for its pleasant climate, lush gardens, and vibrant culinary scene.
As a melting pot of cultures, Bangalore attracts people from across India and the world, making it a diverse and cosmopolitan hub.
